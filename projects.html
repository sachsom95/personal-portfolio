Project 4

Visualization of data from accelerometer using kalman filter

February 2018 - September 2018

Data from accelerometer is collected and visualization of the orientation is made while filtering the drift created by constant acceleration due to gravity by means of kalman filters. The project is to be used on toys which will collect data to detect emotions of autistic kids. The above project was to be implemented using neural networks however was restricted due to time constraints. This project was done in Artificial intelligence Lab (AIRLAB) polimi under Professor Andrea Bonarni.

Project 5

Development of a robot capable of winning an expressive performance contest

February 2018 - September 2018

Development of a robot which needs to express various emotions based on stimuli. The project was part of course Robotics and design attended at Polimi and required an interdisciplinary team of engineers ,designers and management students to develop a bot from its design to final fabrication pipeline.
